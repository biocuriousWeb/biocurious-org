We'll make good use of consumables, gear, and reagents.
Consider also donating to our friends at 
[BioLink Depot](https://www.biolinkdepot.org/).
They're a good place to donate tips, tubes, petri plates, and gear usable
by high school teachers (and others!), however they don't take reagents.
We'll take (almost) anything, except for waste, hazardous waste, and hazardous
reagents that we don't have the capacity or community interest to store and
use effectively.
Additionally if you've got extra of something like HPLC columns, your average
high school doesn't have one running but we do, so contact us and we'd be 
happy to use it to support one of our community projects (or perhaps our 
upcoming HPLC Basics class????)

Examples of what we want:
- FBS

Examples of what we don't want:
- fluorescent bulbs
- cadmium chloride
- HPLC waste
